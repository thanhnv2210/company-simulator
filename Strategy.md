# 🧭 Product Strategy

## 1. Problem
Software engineers are highly skilled in building systems but often lack:
- Understanding of company operations
- Experience making cross-functional decisions
- Awareness of trade-offs between business and engineering

---

## 2. Goal
Build a system that allows engineers to:
- Explore company structure
- Simulate workflows
- Make decisions and see consequences

---

## 3. Target User
- Mid–Senior Software Engineers
- Aspiring Tech Leads / Architects
- Builders who want to launch their own product

---

## 4. Approach

### Phase 1: Non-AI (Foundation)
- Static + rule-based simulation
- Focus on clarity and usability

### Phase 2: Semi-AI
- AI-assisted explanations
- Decision suggestions

### Phase 3: AI-native
- Role simulation (CEO/CTO/PM)
- Personalized company simulation

---

## 5. Core Principle
> Start simple → Ensure daily usefulness → Add intelligence later

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- Personal daily usage
- Decision exploration frequency
- Time spent per session
